Pulang Lupa Day

On September 13th, come hike up to the Pulang Lupa shrine and see come know the history of one of the battles during the Filipino-American war that the local people won over 100 years ago.

Holy Week in Marinduque

Come join in before Easter Sunday. The Moriones Festival, where people dress up as Roman Soldiers, and based on Saint Longinus, a Roman Centorion who is blind in one eye. Come enjoy the festivities.

Christmas

Come visit during the Holiday Season and explore the 6 towns. All with wonderful decorations and activities during Christmas time! As we remember the Savior's birth and how important He is in our lives. You wouldn't want to miss it.
